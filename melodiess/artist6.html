About Ayra Starr

Ayra Starr, born Oyinkansola Sarah Aderibigbe on June 14, 2002, is a rising star in the Nigerian music industry. Hailing from Nigeria, she burst onto the music scene with her unique talent and captivating voice.

Her music style can be described as a fusion of Afrobeat, Afropop, and R&B, with soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics. Ayra Starr's songs often explore themes of love, self-discovery, and personal growth, making her music relatable to a wide audience.

One of her breakthrough moments came with her self-titled debut EP, "Ayra Starr," which featured hit tracks like "Away" and "Sare." The EP received critical acclaim and quickly established her as one of the most promising young artists in Nigeria.

Ayra Starr's rise to fame has been meteoric, and she continues to garner attention and accolades for her exceptional talent. With a bright future ahead of her, she is poised to make a significant impact on the African music scene and beyond.
