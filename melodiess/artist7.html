About Kiss Daniel

Kiss Daniel, whose real name is Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe, is a renowned Nigerian musician and songwriter. Born on May 1, 1990, in Abeokuta, Nigeria, he rose to fame with his distinctive style and captivating music.

He gained widespread recognition in the Nigerian music industry with hits like "Woju," "Laye," and "Mama." His music is a fusion of Afrobeat, Afropop, and R&B, characterized by catchy melodies and meaningful lyrics.

Kiss Daniel's talent and charisma have earned him numerous awards and accolades, making him one of the leading artists in the African music scene. He continues to create music that resonates with fans both in Nigeria and beyond.

With a unique blend of musical prowess and a charismatic stage presence, Kiss Daniel remains a prominent figure in the ever-evolving world of African music.
