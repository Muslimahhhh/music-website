About Zinoleesky

Zinoleesky, whose real name is Oniyide Azeez, is a rising star in the Nigerian music scene. Born on November 21, 1995, in Lagos, Nigeria, he has quickly gained recognition for his unique musical style and compelling songs.

Zinoleesky's music is a fusion of Afrobeat, Afropop, and street-hop, characterized by catchy melodies and heartfelt lyrics. His breakout hit "Ma Pariwo" garnered widespread acclaim and showcased his ability to create music that resonates with a broad audience.

He has collaborated with other prominent artists and continues to release successful singles, including "Kilofeshe" and "Naira Marley." Zinoleesky's authentic storytelling and relatable lyrics often touch on themes of love, struggle, and street life.

Zinoleesky's rapid rise in the Nigerian music industry is a testament to his talent and dedication to his craft. With his unique sound and growing fan base, he is poised to become a significant figure in the Nigerian music scene.
