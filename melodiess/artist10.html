About Mohbad

Mohbad, whose real name is Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba, is a talented Nigerian musician making waves in the music industry. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, he has quickly gained recognition for his unique style and lyrical prowess.

Mohbad's music is characterized by a fusion of Afrobeat and street-hop, and he is known for his energetic and catchy songs. His breakout hit "KPK (Ko Por Ke)" became an anthem in Nigeria and showcased his ability to create music that resonates with the youth.

He has collaborated with other prominent artists and has released several successful singles, including "Marlians Anthem" and "Feel Good." His raw and relatable lyrics often touch on themes of street life and personal experiences.

Mohbad's rising popularity in the Nigerian music scene is a testament to his talent and dedication to his craft. With his unique sound and growing fan base, he is poised to become a significant figure in the Nigerian music industry.
