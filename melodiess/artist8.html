About Davido

Davido, whose real name is David Adedeji Adeleke, is a Nigerian music sensation who has taken the African and global music industry by storm. Born on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, USA, Davido has made a name for himself with his chart-topping hits and charismatic presence.

Known for his Afrobeat and Afropop music, Davido has released numerous successful albums and singles, including "Fall," "If," and "Assurance." His music is celebrated for its infectious beats, catchy lyrics, and the fusion of Nigerian and international musical influences.

In addition to his musical achievements, Davido is also a record producer and entrepreneur. He founded Davido Music Worldwide (DMW), a record label that has nurtured several talented artists in the Nigerian music scene.

Davido's contributions to the music industry have earned him numerous awards and recognition both in Africa and around the world. His energetic performances and commitment to his craft have solidified his position as one of Africa's most influential and respected musicians.
